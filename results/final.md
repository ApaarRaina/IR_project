## IV. CRITICAL PATIENT SAFETY GUIDELINES

### Before Admission:
1. **Research the specific doctor**, not just the hospital
2. **Get written cost estimates** with breakdown of all charges
3. **Verify doctor availability** and backup plans
4. **Check recent reviews** (within last 6 months)
5. **Confirm insurance acceptance** in writing
6. **Ask about ICU protocols** and doctor visit frequency

### Red Flags - Leave Immediately If:
- Doctor refuses to listen or is dismissive
- Sudden procedure additions without clear explanation
- Staff unable to explain treatment plan
- Billing estimates change drastically without justification
- Family members not allowed proper communication
- ICU patients left unattended for hours
- Pressure to do immediate procedures without second opinion time

### Documentation Requirements:
- Record all doctor communications
- Keep copies of all bills and estimates
- Document medication schedules
- Note names of attending doctors and nurses
- Photograph any consent forms before signing
- Keep discharge summary and all reports
